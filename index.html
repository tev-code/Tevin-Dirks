About-Me

My name is Tevin Dirks and I am 26 years of age. I currently work in the hospitality industry as a waitron, my coding journey began in October 2019 when I decided to enrol onto one of Hyperion Developments bootcamps. I decided to do the full stack web-development bootcamp due to its dynamic skill set offered, so I am able to learn both front-end and back-end development. Before my coding journey I did music production, at first everything I learnt was self-taught until I decided to do a short course(3 months) in music production using FL studio through the SAE institute. A year later I felt I needed to build my CV arsenal and my personal skill set, after some research and new interests I realised that coding is the skill that intrigued me the most.
